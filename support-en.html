FAQ

Q. My notes disappeared

If iCloud sync is enabled in Settings, your notes can be recovered from iCloud Documents. Restart the app to trigger automatic sync.

Q. AI features are not working

Please check your internet connection. AI features require a server connection. If you've exceeded the free usage limit, a Pro subscription provides additional usage.

Q. Can I import Obsidian notes?

Yes. Go to Settings > Import from Obsidian and select your Obsidian vault folder to batch import .md files.

Q. How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. After cancellation, Pro features remain available until the end of the billing period.

Q. How do I delete all my data?

Use Settings > Delete Account in the app to remove server data. Deleting the app removes all local data from your device.
