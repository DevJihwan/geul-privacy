Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-09

Geul ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage your data.

1. Information Collected

  • Device ID (UUID) — A unique identifier generated per device, not linked to your identity.
  • User ID — A server-generated identifier for managing your account and usage.
  • AI usage data — Daily and monthly request counts for rate limiting.
  • Subscription status — To provide appropriate service tiers.
  • iCloud sync data — Your notes are stored in Apple iCloud Documents if sync is enabled. We do not access this data.

2. AI Features

When you use AI features, the content of your note is sent to our Cloudflare Workers server for processing.

Your content is processed via Anthropic's Claude API and immediately discarded. We do not store your note content on our servers.

AI features are entirely optional. If you do not use them, your note content is never sent to any server.

3. Data Storage

  • Notes — Stored locally on your device + optionally in iCloud Documents.
  • Authentication tokens — Stored securely in the device Keychain only.
  • Server — Only device_id, user_id, and usage data are stored in Cloudflare KV.

4. Data Deletion

  • Settings > Delete Account — Permanently deletes all server data.
  • Deleting the app removes all local data from your device.
  • iCloud data can be managed through Apple's iCloud settings on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI text processing. Content is not stored.
  • Apple (iCloud, StoreKit) — Cloud sync and in-app purchases.
  • Cloudflare (Workers, KV) — API server and usage data storage.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@geul.app.
